Some facts

Things you probably didn't know about Taylor Swift

Cats

Taylor has three cats. Two of the cats are named after famous TV characters. The cats, Meredith and Olivia, are named after Ellen Pompeo's character in Grey's Anatomy, whilst Olivia gets her name from Mariska Hargitay's character in Law & Order: Special Victims. Taylor's third and newest cat is called Benjamin Button, after Brad Pitt's character in the film of the same name.

Records

When Taylor's sixth album, 'reputation' was released in 2017, she became the only artist to release four consecutive albums which sold more than one million copies in their first week of release. In July 2023, Taylor's song 'Anti-Hero surpassed a billion streams on Spotify. Taylor also has three other tracks in the Billions Club - 'Shake It Off', 'Blank Space' and her collab with Zayn Malik, 'I Don't Wanna Live Forever'.

Pseudonym

Taylor normally writes under her own name, but once she used the pseudonym Nils Sjöberg on Calvin Harris' song 'This Is What You Came For' featuring Rihanna. At the time, Calvin and Taylor were dating and it's believed that they didn't want their names to overshadow the song's release.
